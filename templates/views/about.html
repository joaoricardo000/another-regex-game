Another Regex Game

Heavly inspired by the many great regex games that already existed, like

Regex Golf and Regex Crosswords

What is it?

A game about finding patterns and creating expressions for words validation.

What?

You create regular expressions to analyze words letter by letter, and validates it based on rules.

...?

You teach your computer to analyse words! So much fun!

How can I learn more about regular expressions?

The internet is full of content for all skill level, just look for it. A good place to start is the wikipedia article.

How can I save and share a level?

You can't really save, just share the url.

It's a good idea to share using #regexgame, so for the first time you will use a hashtag to store and index similar content for a quick search.

How the points on golf are calculated?

Every good match +10 points, bad match -10.

For every character in regex -1 point and -5 for each | .
